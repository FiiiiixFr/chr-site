Welcome to CHR Solution

Why we built CHR Solution, and what we want to change in the daily life of restaurateurs and distributors.

If you work in hospitality, you know the scene. An Excel file named "supplier-prices-2024-v3-final-ok.xlsx". A paper binder of orders. A saturated inbox with PDF mercurial lists, scanned invoices and "can you confirm the exact quantity?".

Restaurants are demanding. Food distribution is too. And yet, digital tools are often a generation behind — or ill-fitted, because built for other verticals.

Our bet

CHR Solution starts from a conviction: purchasing and stock, in hospitality, are one single flow. When the distributor updates a price, the restaurateur\'s recipe should know. When the restaurateur orders, the sheet lands at the distributor — not in a new email slot.

What\'s coming

We\'re just getting started. On our roadmap: E-signature module, invoice OCR (Mindee), AI for allergen detection and calorie computation.

If you want to be part of the journey — write to us.

— The CHR Solution team
