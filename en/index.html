CHR POS

Modern point of sale for cafés, hotels and restaurants. iPad, Android tablet and pocket. NF525 compliant.

  • Multi-method checkout (card, cash, vouchers, cheques)
  • Floor plan, table transfers, split bills
  • Multi-printer (cold kitchen, hot kitchen, bar, tickets)
  • Offline mode with auto-sync on reconnect
  • ECDSA-signed Z closure + 6-year archives
Learn more →

CHR Stock

For restaurateurs who want to manage stock, recipes and food-cost without spreadsheets.

  • Fast inventories on mobile or tablet
  • Recipes with spec sheets and food-cost
  • Sales, losses, inter-site transfers
  • Production, manufacturing, sub-recipes
  • Analytics: food-cost, variances, plate margin

CHR Purchasing

For distributors, wholesalers and purchasing managers who push their mercurial lists to customers.

  • Master mercurial by supplier and family
  • Distribute prices and products to customers
  • Centralized, tracked customer orders
  • Rich supplier cards (contacts, terms, products)
  • Printable purchase sheet for offline customers
