Features

A full platform
for hospitality.

Three modules designed together: POS, stock, purchasing. Use them separately or combined. Shared data, no double entry.

CHR POS

Modern point of sale for cafés, hotels and restaurants. iPad, Android tablet, pocket. NF525 compliant.

  • Multi-method checkout (card, cash, vouchers, cheques)
  • Floor plan, table transfers, split bills
  • Multi-printer (kitchen, bar, tickets)
  • Offline mode with auto-sync
  • ECDSA-signed Z closure + 6-year archives
  • Android Pocket for servers (add-on)
See details →

CHR Stock

Restaurant-trade module: inventories, recipes, sales, production, analytics.

  • Mobile inventories, offline-compatible
  • Recipes and sub-recipes
  • Real-time food-cost
  • Sales, losses, inter-site transfers
  • Production / manufacturing
  • AI Allergens & Calories (add-on)
See details →

CHR Purchasing

Supplier-side & buyer-side module: mercurial, reference products, suppliers, orders.

  • Mercurial by supplier and family
  • Reference products (purchase ↔ sale)
  • Complete supplier card
  • Centralized customer orders
  • PDF purchase sheet printing
  • Mindee invoice OCR (add-on)
