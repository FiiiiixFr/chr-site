Checkout

Table layout

Table layout, tables, servers

Drag-and-drop tables on a visual map. State at a glance: free, occupied, awaiting check, to be cleared. Transfer between tables, split bills, server tracking on each line.

Checkout modes

Multiple methods, split payments

Card, cash, restaurant ticket, cheque — all configurable. Native split payment, round-amount shortcuts, two-tap bill split. Signed fiscal receipt prints on the spot.

Bill draft + NF525

One bill for the table, one fiscal receipt for the till

Bill2Step: hand the customer a non-fiscal draft bill first (clearly marked "TEMPORARY BILL — NOT A FISCAL RECEIPT"), then only after payment is the final fiscal receipt printed, cryptographically signed (ECDSA P-256). Z closure in one tap, Z archive kept 6 years, NF525 / BOI-TVA-DECLA-30-10-30 compliant. Tax audits go through smoothly.
