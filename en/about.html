About

Hospitality deserves
better than a spreadsheet.

CHR Solution was born from a simple observation: despite general digitalization, restaurateurs and their distributors still exchange Excel files, paper orders and email attachments. We can do much better.

Our mission

Connect the two trades of hospitality — purchasing and stock — in a single fluid platform. Offer restaurateurs a tool worthy of their kitchen standards. Offer distributors a modern customer portal.

Our approach

We build with hospitality professionals. Every feature is born from a real case, tested in production, adjusted. No gadget features, no disguised generic ERP.

Our values

  • Simplicity first — a tool that doesn\'t justify itself by complexity.
  • Transparency — clear prices, readable ToS, no vendor lock-in.
  • Proximity — human support, in French & English, that knows the trade.

Where we are

CHR Solution is a French startup. Our hosting, our support, our data — all in Europe.

Demo, partnership, press — we take the time.
